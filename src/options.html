Please enter the credentials for this site (TISS) below and afterwards don't forget to enable the extension. You can disable it by clicking on the extension's icon in the upper right corner of your browser.

Attention:

Your credentials are synchronized between your chrome instances. They will be slightly obfuscated and need to be stored locally as well.

Please note that sadly, there is no way for this extension to safely encrypt your data. So keep people away from your machine!
